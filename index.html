Help Mini Sharkie to find his way through the world of Mini Sharkies Adventure!
Collect coins to get stronger, collect hearts to gain life and collect poison for throwing them at enemies - but be careful: the endboss can only be beaten with poison, so don´t waste them!
Avoid collisions with enemies. Slap them for beating them.

How to move Mini Sharkie

P - Pause game
A or ← - Move left
D or → - Move right
W or ↑ - Move up
S or ↓ - Move down
SPACE - Slap
C - Shoot bubble
LEFT SHIFT - Swim fast
